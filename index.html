Eviction Prevention

The app that helps landlords/holding companies find jobs for there Tenants.

  • Find Jobs For Others:

    We want to make it easy for landlords, holding companys, non profets or a friend to find a job for another person. This app will provide the tools for others to access a persons job search information in order to apply for jobs, edit resumes, and edit job search profiles provided that the individual looking for work has approved such access.

  • Job Seekers:

    On the other hand anybody can sign up for a free account and use the software to find work. This app will utilize tools that will allow for the user to have one place to edit all of their personal job search websites such as Indeed, Monster or LinkedIn.

  • Site Abilities:

    Tools on the site will allow for easy AI generated cover letters, as well as auto fill/complete application process to make it easy & quick when applying to jobs. The app will keep track of what has and has not been applied to. It will provide follow up alerts with companies contact info or posible ways to find the information on how to reach a business.

  • Recruiting:

    Recruiters will find this app useful in finding potential candidates for job openings in their company. In this app we hope to integrate a Google API that allows for an AI algorithm to better match candidates to a specific job.
